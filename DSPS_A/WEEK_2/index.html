Zoo Antwerp

Welcome to the captivating world of the Antwerp Zoo!
Nestled in the heart of the picturesque Belgian city of Antwerp, our zoo has been a cherished destination for nature enthusiasts, families, and wildlife aficionados for over a century. With a rich history dating back to 1843, the Antwerp Zoo has continuously evolved, setting new standards in animal care, conservation, and education. As you embark on this digital journey through our virtual gates, we invite you to explore the wonders of the animal kingdom, discover our commitment to conservation, and learn about the diverse array of species that call our zoo home. Whether you're planning a visit or simply seeking a window into the enchanting realm of wildlife, the Antwerp Zoo website is your gateway to a world of fascination and adventure.
